# PROMO'S UNICORNS — DESIGN.MD

## Overview

**Fantasy Football · Est. 2004 · Season 22**

> *Once a Meme. Now a Myth.*

### The Story

Promo's Unicorns is a 21-season fantasy football team with zero championships. Previously known as the Promo Predators, the team rebranded to own the narrative: the unicorn — the internet's symbol for something that doesn't exist — became the brand. The pink is not apology. The pink is armor.

This is a premium sports identity built on self-awareness and competitive hunger simultaneously. The design system must feel like a real NFL franchise: collectible, bold, and worth belonging to. Every asset should look like it came from the same locker room.

### Brand Positioning

| Axis | Position |
|---|---|
| Tone | Competitive but self-aware |
| Prestige | Premium collectible — franchise-grade |
| Humor | Earned, not performed |
| Aspiration | The ring is coming. Someday. |

**Primary tagline:** Once a Meme. Now a Myth.
**Secondary tagline:** Born to Lose. Built to Reign.

### Personality

The UI and copy should feel like five things at once: **mythically confident**, **sharply self-aware**, **trophy hungry**, **community-coded**, and **collector-grade**. Think of the Buffalo Bills' underdog electricity or the Cubs pre-2016 — decades of futility turned into identity gold. The humor and the ambition are the same thing, not opposites.

### Brand Voice

Copy must be sharp and earned, never try-hard. Key tones by context:

- **Matchday:** "Horns up. The myth continues today."
- **After a win:** "The myth wins. [score]. Another chapter."
- **After a loss:** "The legend grows. Added to the lore." — never defeated; unicorns don't do defeated.
- **Trash talk:** "The horn is not decorative."
- **Draft Day:** "This is the year. (We say this every year. This time we mean it.)"
- **Championship week:** "21 years of impossible. One week to mythical."

**Primary hashtag:** `#PromosUnicorns` — on every public-facing asset.
**Rally cry:** `#HornsUp` — matchday and motivation content.
**Campaign tags:** `#BornToLoseBuiltToReign` · `#MythicalSeason` · `#YearOfTheUnicorn`

## Colors

Colors extracted directly from the official mascot illustration and logo lockup assets.

The palette is built on two anchors and one aspirational accent.

- **Primary — Unicorn Pink (`#E8185A`):** The defining mark of the rebrand. Hot magenta-pink sourced from the mascot's mane. Used for CTAs, key type accents, jersey numbers, and all primary brand statements. This color is the rebrand in physical form: loud, unapologetic, and impossible to ignore.

- **Secondary — Dynasty Purple (`#1E1040`):** Near-black deep purple sourced from the mascot's outlines and logo wordmark. The team's foundation color — authority, premium depth, and maximum contrast. The primary dark surface for all layouts.

- **Tertiary — Victory Gold (`#F2C200`):** Sourced from the mascot's horn and glasses lenses. Strictly reserved for championship, aspirational, and limited-edition contexts. Overuse kills its meaning. Every time Victory Gold appears, it signals something rare is happening.

- **Neutral — Blush Pink (`#F0A8C0`):** The brand's ambient warmth. The background color of the official logo assets. Softer than the primary pink; used for page backgrounds, card surfaces, the away jersey base, and light-mode UI.

- **Surface (`#FDF0F5`):** Warm off-white with a pink undertone. Softer than pure white, more refined. Used for document backgrounds, light UI containers, and premium merch backgrounds.

- **Shadow (`#4B1B70`):** Mid-tone purple sourced from the mascot's mane depth layers. Used for secondary dark backgrounds and depth layering.

- **On-Primary / On-Secondary (`#FFFFFF`):** Myth White — maximum contrast surface, the unicorn's face. Used for reversed logos, type on dark surfaces, and the home jersey base.

- **On-Tertiary / On-Neutral (`#1E1040`):** Dynasty Purple as foreground on gold and blush surfaces.

| Background | Foreground | Ratio | Level |
|---|---|---|---|
| `#1E1040` | `#FFFFFF` | 16.8:1 | AAA ✅ |
| `#1E1040` | `#E8185A` | 4.7:1 | AA ✅ |
| `#E8185A` | `#FFFFFF` | 4.7:1 | AA ✅ |
| `#F0A8C0` | `#1E1040` | 7.1:1 | AAA ✅ |
| `#F2C200` | `#1E1040` | 10.4:1 | AAA ✅ |

### Home / Away / Alternate Color Schemes

- **Home — "Dynasty Night":** Secondary base + Primary accent + Tertiary highlight. Dark, premium, intimidating.
- **Away — "Unicorn Blush":** Neutral base + Secondary accent + Primary detail. The opponent sees pink and underestimates. That is the point.
- **Alternate — "Ring Season":** Tertiary base + Secondary accent. Activated only when the team reaches championship week. Scarcity is intentional.

## Typography

Two typefaces only. No exceptions.

**Barlow Condensed** (Google Fonts, free) is the display and headline face for all brand applications. Its athletic condensed structure matches the existing logo lockup letterforms exactly. Used exclusively at ExtraBold (800) and Bold (700) weight, always ALL CAPS. The narrow set width allows for dramatic scale without sacrificing hierarchy.

**Inter** (Google Fonts, free) is the body and UI face for all supporting text. Its neutral geometry keeps the reading layer clean so Barlow Condensed can dominate visually. Used at Regular (400), Medium (500), and SemiBold (600).

### Scale Rationale

- **`display`:** Reserved for hero moments — campaign headlines, poster type, score readouts. Use once per composition maximum.
- **`headline-lg`:** Primary section headers, team name lock-up contexts, matchday graphic headers.
- **`headline-md`:** Sub-section headers, card titles, UI page titles.
- **`subheading`:** Category labels in condensed ALL CAPS with positive tracking. Bridge between display and body.
- **`label-caps`:** Inter at micro scale, heavily tracked. Used for ALL CAPS eyebrow labels, button text, badge text, metadata. The `letterSpacing: 0.15em` is mandatory — tighter reads as an error.
- **`body-md` / `body-sm`:** All prose. Never Barlow Condensed for running text.
- **`caption`:** Timestamps, attribution, fine print.
- **`stat-display`:** Barlow Condensed for large numeral display — scores, stats, season counts. Victory Gold (`{colors.tertiary}`) is the default foreground color for this token.

### Rules

- Maximum three type sizes in any single composition.
- Barlow Condensed appears only in `display`, `headline-lg`, `headline-md`, `subheading`, and `stat-display` tokens.
- `label-caps` is the only Inter variant used in ALL CAPS contexts.
- Do not use Barlow Condensed italic in any brand application.
- Do not use font weights below 700 for Barlow Condensed in any brand context.

## Layout

The layout follows a content-first, high-contrast column model with a strict 8px base unit. All spacing derives from multiples of `{spacing.sm}` (8px).

### Grid

- **Digital (social / web):** Single column for mobile; two or three columns for dashboard/desktop. Max content width: 1200px. Gutters: `{spacing.gutter}` (24px).
- **Social canvases:** Fixed at 1080×1080px (square) and 1080×1350px (portrait). All elements snap to the 8px grid.
- **Print (posters, banners):** 18×24in at 300dpi. Two-column grid with `{spacing.2xl}` (56px) margins.

### Composition Principles

Layouts use dramatic scale contrast as the primary organizing principle. One large display element dominates; everything else is subordinate. Empty space makes the dominant element hit harder.

- **Hero:** Full-width `{colors.secondary}` surface. `display` type anchored bottom-left. Mascot upper-right, partial crop. Unicorn Pink rule (4px) below headline. Stat row in Victory Gold at bottom.
- **Card:** `{rounded.lg}` container. `{spacing.lg}` internal padding. Dark or light surface only.
- **Social post:** Edge-to-edge color fill. Mascot always upper-right, partially cropped. Type anchored bottom-left. `{colors.primary}` bottom bar (8px height) with `label-caps` hashtag.

## Elevation & Depth

Depth is achieved through **tonal layering and color contrast**, not shadows. This is a flat, high-contrast design system. Drop shadows are prohibited throughout.

### Layer Stack

| Layer | Token | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Page / Canvas | `{colors.surface}` or `{colors.neutral}` | Base surface |
| Card / Container | `{colors.secondary}` or `#FFFFFF` | Content grouping |
| Elevated element | `{colors.shadow}` | Secondary depth |
| Accent bar / rule | `{colors.primary}` | Visual anchor, separator |
| Foreground / Focal | `{colors.tertiary}` type or mascot | Highest-priority element |

### Depth in the Mascot

The mascot itself embeds the depth system: Myth White face sits above the Blush Pink neck tones, in front of the Unicorn Pink mane, layered over Shadow Purple depth strokes, all contained by the Dynasty Purple outline. When placing the mascot in a composition, its built-in depth hierarchy must not compete with other layered elements.

### Social Post Depth

Result posts use a diagonal split — `{colors.secondary}` and `{colors.primary}` — to signal energy and divide win/loss contexts visually without photography. Trash talk posts use a single flat `{colors.primary}` field; the copy does all the work.

## Shapes

The shape language is defined by **bold geometry with controlled softness**. Sharp corners signal competition and authority. Minimal rounding signals premium craft. Nothing is fully rounded except badge pills.

- **`{rounded.none}` (0px):** Jersey graphics, banner backgrounds, full-bleed color fields, divider lines.
- **`{rounded.sm}` (4px):** Buttons, input fields, small chips. Default interactive element radius.
- **`{rounded.md}` (8px):** Stat cards, tooltip containers, image crop frames.
- **`{rounded.lg}` (12px):** Content cards, social post containers, merch mockup frames.
- **`{rounded.full}` (9999px):** Badge pills only — week labels, WIN/LOSS tags, category chips.

### Geometric Language

The mascot's shield base is the brand's primary shape archetype: a roughly pentagonal form tapering at the bottom, rooted in classic sports heraldry. The horn introduces a sharp upward diagonal as a secondary motif — used sparingly in dividers, crop angles, and layout cuts.

The only diagonal permitted in layout compositions runs **lower-left to upper-right**, matching the horn's direction. Do not introduce arbitrary diagonal cuts.

### Logo Shield Rule

The mascot's shield base must never be redrawn or distorted. It is a fixed asset reproduced from master files only. Its internal geometry is the brand's most concentrated visual statement.

## Components

### Buttons

- **`button-primary`:** Unicorn Pink fill, white `label-caps` text. The single most important action on any screen. Used once per view maximum.
- **`button-primary-hover`:** Darkens to `#B5124A`. Transition at 150ms maximum.
- **`button-secondary`:** Dynasty Purple fill, white `label-caps` text. Supporting actions and navigation.
- **`button-secondary-hover`:** Lightens to `#2E1449`. Transition at 150ms maximum.

### Badges and Pills

- **`badge`:** Unicorn Pink pill with `{rounded.full}`. Used for `WEEK 00` labels, category tags, status indicators. Always fully rounded — never rectangular.
- **`badge-gold`:** Victory Gold pill with Dynasty Purple text. Reserved exclusively for `WIN`, championship, or achievement-tier indicators. Do not use for routine labels.

### Cards

- **`card-dark`:** Dynasty Purple surface, white text, `{rounded.lg}`. Primary content container in dark layouts. Optional Unicorn Pink top accent border: `3px solid {colors.primary}`.
- **`card-light`:** Surface off-white, Dynasty Purple text. For document, light UI, and print contexts.
- **`stat-card`:** Dynasty Purple surface, Victory Gold `stat-display` type. Three per row maximum. Displays season counts, scores, records.

### Navigation

- **`nav`:** Dynasty Purple bar, 64px height, white `label-caps` links. Unicorn Pink active indicator (3px bottom border). No rounded corners — edge-to-edge.

### Social Media Templates

All social templates snap to the 8px grid. Square: 1080×1080px. Portrait: 1080×1350px.

- **`social-matchday`:** `{colors.secondary}` field. `headline-lg` matchup text bottom-left. `badge` week number upper-left. Mascot upper-right, partial crop. `{colors.primary}` bottom bar (8px) with `#PromosUnicorns` in `label-caps`.
- **`social-result-win`:** `{colors.secondary}` + `{colors.primary}` diagonal split. `headline-lg` score in Myth White. `badge-gold` WIN label. Assertive mascot crop.
- **`social-result-loss`:** Full `{colors.secondary}` field. Score in `{colors.neutral}` type — never in primary or tertiary. Standard mascot position. Copy reframes loss as lore. No defeat posture.
- **`social-trash-talk`:** Full `{colors.primary}` field. Single `headline-lg` or `headline-md` line in Myth White, centered. `PU` monogram badge lower-right. No mascot — the copy is the entire design.

**Copy templates:**

| Post type | Copy format |
|---|---|
| Matchday | `WEEK [00] · PU vs. [OPPONENT] · HORNS UP.` |
| Win | `[SCORE] · THE MYTH WINS. · ANOTHER CHAPTER.` |
| Loss | `[SCORE] · THE LEGEND GROWS. · ADDED TO THE LORE.` |
| Trash talk | Single bold line. No explanation. |

### Jerseys

All jersey components use `{rounded.none}` — all edges sharp. Number font: `headline-lg` at 48pt minimum. Name plate: `subheading` at `letterSpacing: 0.1em`.

- **`jersey-home` — "Dynasty Night":** `{colors.secondary}` body. `{colors.primary}` numbers outlined in `{colors.tertiary}`. Myth White name plate. Double side stripe: Primary outer + Tertiary inner.
- **`jersey-away` — "Unicorn Blush":** `{colors.neutral}` body. `{colors.secondary}` numbers with `{colors.primary}` shadow. The opponent underestimates the pink. That is the point.
- **`jersey-alternate` — "Ring Season":** `{colors.tertiary}` body. `{colors.secondary}` numbers. Activated only at championship week — scarcity is intentional.

Hidden details shared across all jerseys: horn icon embroidered on back collar tab · `EST. 2004` on inner neckline tape · `S22` on right sleeve hem · `PU` monogram on waistband.

## Do's and Don'ts

- Do lead with the mascot — it is the strongest and most differentiated visual asset in the system
- Do use `{colors.secondary}` as the primary dark surface in all layouts
- Do apply `{colors.primary}` at full saturation — it is never toned down
- Do reserve `{colors.tertiary}` exclusively for championship, aspirational, or limited-edition contexts
- Do use dramatic type scale contrast — one `display` element dominates each composition
- Do apply `{rounded.full}` to badge pills and `{rounded.lg}` to content cards consistently
- Do maintain `letterSpacing: 0.15em` on all `label-caps` applications without exception
- Do produce all digital assets at 2× resolution minimum
- Do include `#PromosUnicorns` on every public-facing social asset
- Do use `badge-gold` only when something genuinely championship-tier is being communicated

- Don't apologize for `{colors.primary}` — it is the brand's armor, not its weakness
- Don't introduce colors outside the defined token set in any application
- Don't apply drop shadows, glows, bevel effects, or gradients to any brand element
- Don't use Barlow Condensed in body copy or at weights below 700
- Don't flip the mascot — it always faces right
- Don't combine more than three brand colors in a single composition
- Don't use `badge-gold` for routine labeling — scarcity of the gold signal is the point
- Don't introduce diagonal cuts in any direction other than lower-left to upper-right
- Don't set interactive state transitions slower than 200ms or faster than 100ms
- Don't over-explain the brand's humor in copy — the myth and the ambition are the same statement
